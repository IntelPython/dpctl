######
Device
######

.. contents::
   :local:
   :backlinks: none
   :depth: 1

The :class:`dpctl.SyclDevice` class is an abstract representation of a
data-parallel computation device. A device can be a CPU, GPU, FPGA, or other
type of accelerator.
